Who We Are?

The Robotics Club CMC Rabat is a passionate community of students and tech enthusiasts exploring robotics, automation, and AI. We foster innovation, creativity, and problem-solving through hands-on projects, interactive workshops, and exciting competitions.

By working on real-world challenges, members gain practical experience in robotics design, coding, and AI-driven automation. Join us to develop cutting-edge skills, collaborate with like-minded innovators, and shape the future of technology!
